A Solution for all types of traders

Three modules viz., Purchases, Inventory and Sales to work out all traders issues.

  • Purchases is the flow from purchase orders, Material inwards, Purchase Returns and Supplier payments
  • Inventory is to maintain samples, losses and physical stock auditing
  • Sales is the flow from Sale orders, Dispatches & invoicing, Sale Returns and Customer receipts
